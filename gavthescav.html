G.A.V The Scav

This collaborative project was the other element to my Final Major Project, in which I worked with two fellow developers as well as three Artists, over an eight week period. This game ‘G.A.V The Scav’ is a First-Person shooter in a science fiction setting. I lead the team as the sole AI Programmer, using C++ where applicable. I utilised behaviour trees (with custom task nodes and decorators), and animation blueprints in conjunction with backend AI programming. As I was also the team manager I filled roles as a general programming aid when needed.
